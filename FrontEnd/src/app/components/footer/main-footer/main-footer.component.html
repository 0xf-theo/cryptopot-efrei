About us

CryptoPot is a website that allows users to make charitable donations in stablecoins such as BUSD, USDC, USDT, and DAI. It provides a simple and secure platform for users to donate to their chosen organizations and track the use of their donations. With CryptoPot, users can support the causes they care about while also taking advantage of the stability and security of stablecoins for their transactions.
