How to donate

Giving on CryptoPot is easy and secure. Once you've found the organization and cause that you want to support, you can find the donation form on their page. On the form, you can specify the amount you want to donate and choose which stablecoin you want to use (BUSD, USDC, USDT, or DAI). To make a donation, all you need is a wallet that supports the stablecoin you've chosen. From there, simply enter your wallet address and the amount you want to donate, and your donation will be on its way. The platform will then provide you with a confirmation of your donation.

Tax receipt

Regarding tax receipts, please note that the option is available on our platform. If you wish to receive a tax receipt, you will be prompted to provide an email address and the organization will contact you to proceed. It's important to check with your local tax authorities to determine if your donation is tax-deductible and how to proceed.

Wallet setup

To make a donation on CryptoPot, you will need to send the funds to an address displayed on the donation form. This address is specific to the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network. When using MetaMask, you will need to ensure that you have selected BSC as the network and have inputted the correct RPC URL, chain ID, and explorer URL.

The RPC URL for BSC is https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
The Chain ID for BSC is 56
The explorer URL for BSC is https://bscscan.com/

Once you have these details set up in MetaMask, you can input the donation address displayed on the donation form, specify the amount you want to donate, and send the funds. You will then receive a confirmation of your donation on the platform.

Please note that it is very important to double-check the address you're sending to, as well as the network and the correct chain ID before making any transaction, as sending to the wrong address can result in a loss of funds.

Confirmation of the transaction

When making a donation on CryptoPot, it's important to note that the transaction will not be immediately confirmed. Instead, it will first need to be included in a block on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) blockchain. This process can take a few minutes, during which time the transaction will be in a "pending" state.

Once the transaction has been included in a block, it will need to be confirmed by a certain number of additional blocks before it is considered final. The number of confirmations required can vary depending on the network, but typically around 10 confirmations are needed before a transaction is considered fully confirmed on BSC.

Each block on the Binance Smart Chain is mined every 3 sec on average, which means that it will take around 30 sec to mine 10 blocks. Once the transaction has been confirmed by the required number of blocks, it will be considered final and the funds will be credited to the recipient's address.

It's important to note that the time it takes for a transaction to be confirmed can vary depending on network conditions and the fee attached to the transaction.
